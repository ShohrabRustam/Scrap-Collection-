Waste management solutions we offer to corporate/institutions

Automated Smart Dustbins

To segregate Dry & wet waste, we install an innovative & tech-friendly bins where every information is propagated via our Application system.

Recycling of segregated waste

To establish the recycling process, we convert wet or organic waste into Bio-gas compost to serve some basic essential needs of partnering businesses. The best industrial tools & apparatuses are used to carry out the recycling process.

Paper shredding

Best mechanical tools are utilized to destroy confidential & sensitive documents for tearing them into small pieces & then intended for recycling.

Electronic waste management ( EPR Act)

Under the strict guidelines of E-waste management rules 2016, we engage ourselves in the collection, disassemble & recycling of such electronic Scrap for an extra advantage.

Waste Audit

An entire analysis report of the distinctive sort of waste discharged is done and the redirection rate is computed in weights to quantify the execution of an association's Recycling and Reusing program.

Impact/Sustainability report

We generate a report on, how much waste is collected and what type of waste is collected. From our Smart bins, we demonstrate a positive effect made by an organization in segregation & recycling of waste for a promising environment-friendly future.
